How to Use This Guide

Each use case follows the same structure: What it does, Why it matters, Tools you need, and Real workflow example. Start with the categories most relevant to your current bottleneck. The highest-ROI starting points for most sellers are Dynamic Repricing (#7), Shipment Reconciliation (#21), Buyer Message Triage (#15), and Weekly Business Review (#27).

Chapter 1
🔬 Product Research & Sourcing
Find winning products faster, qualify suppliers automatically, and spot trends before your competitors do.
🎯

1. Opportunity Scout

Product Research

Monitors BSR (Best Seller Rank) shifts, new product launches, and search volume trends across Amazon, Bol.com, and Kaufland daily. Flags categories where top-10 listings are weak — low reviews, poor images, thin content — meaning you can win with a better listing.

Why It Matters
Finds blue-ocean niches before they become red ocean. Saves weeks of manual research.
Tools Needed
Jungle Scout API, Keepa API, Helium 10 API, Claude for scoring logic
🔎

2. Competitor Reverse-Engineering Agent

Product Research

Takes a competitor ASIN/EAN and reverse-engineers their full strategy: supplier (via import records), margin estimate, keyword set, review velocity, and ad spend estimate. Outputs a "should we compete?" scorecard.

Why It Matters
Removes guesswork from competition decisions. Quantifies whether a category is worth entering.
Tools Needed
ImportYeti, Keepa, Helium 10 Cerebro, Claude
🏭

3. Alibaba Supplier Qualifier

Product Research

Given a product spec, scrapes Alibaba/Made-in-China, cross-references supplier ratings, MOQ, certifications (CE, RoHS), and lead times. Sends RFQ emails automatically, collects responses, and ranks suppliers by risk/cost.

Why It Matters
Turns weeks of supplier vetting into hours. Reduces the risk of bad suppliers costing you money.
Tools Needed
Firecrawl, Gmail, Claude, Airtable
📈

4. Trend-to-Product Pipeline

Product Research

Monitors TikTok hashtags, Reddit subs, Pinterest boards, and Google Trends for emerging product trends. When a trend crosses a velocity threshold, auto-generates a product brief with target price point, key features, and first-mover window estimate.

Why It Matters
Catches trends before they hit mainstream. First mover advantage = better BSR, cheaper PPC.
Tools Needed
Firecrawl, Reddit API, Google Trends API, Claude
⚖️

5. Patent & Trademark Clearance Agent

Product Research

Before sourcing a product, checks USPTO/EUIPO for design patents, word marks, and utility patents on the product category. Flags risk items and suggests design-arounds before you invest.

Why It Matters
Prevents costly inventory seizures and legal battles. A €200 search saves a €50,000 mistake.
Tools Needed
USPTO API, EUIPO API, Claude
Chapter 2
💲 Pricing & Margin Management
Protect your margin at scale. Never lose the Buy Box at the wrong price again.
⚡

6. Dynamic Repricing Agent

Pricing

Monitors Buy Box ownership every 15 minutes. Adjusts price using rules: win Buy Box if margin > X%, hold price if already winning, pull back if a competitor goes below your floor. Incorporates your exact landed cost per unit (FBA fees, VAT, ad spend).

Why It Matters
Most repricers use generic rules. This one uses YOUR actual margins. You stop leaving money on the table.
Tools Needed
Amazon SP-API, Bol.com API, your ERP, Claude for edge cases
📉

7. Fee Erosion Detector

Pricing

Runs monthly reconciliation of every Amazon/Bol.com fee line: FBA storage, long-term storage, return fees, referral fees. Flags anomalies vs. prior month and vs. category benchmarks. Identifies products where fees silently ate margins over time.

Why It Matters
Amazon's fees change constantly. Most sellers don't notice until their margin is already gone.
Tools Needed
Amazon SP-API Finance endpoints, Claude for anomaly detection
🔄

8. Cross-Marketplace Price Parity Agent

Pricing

Ensures your prices stay consistent across Amazon DE/NL/FR, Bol.com, and Kaufland to avoid MAP violations or marketplace suspensions. Auto-adjusts when currency fluctuates or a marketplace auto-reprices you.

Why It Matters
One MAP violation can get you suspended. This agent enforces your price floor automatically.
Tools Needed
SP-API, Bol.com Retailer API, Kaufland API, FX rates API
📊

9. Promo ROI Forecaster

Pricing

Before you run a Lightning Deal or Bol.com Spotlight, calculates expected sales lift (using your BSR elasticity curve), ad cost, deal fee, and inventory consumption. Outputs go/no-go with expected net profit delta.

Why It Matters
Many deals lose money. This gives you the numbers before you commit inventory.
Tools Needed
Keepa historical data, your margin sheet, Claude
Chapter 3
✏️ Content & Listing Optimization
Win the algorithm and convert more buyers with listings built by AI that actually understands your product.
✍️

10. Full-Funnel Listing Writer

Content

Creates title, 5 bullets, description, A+ content copy, and backend search terms from: a product spec sheet, top-10 competitor listings, and a keyword universe. Outputs per-marketplace variants (Amazon DE vs. NL vs. FR vs. Bol.com — each with correct character limits and buyer language).

Why It Matters
A great listing is a permanent traffic source. This generates them at scale, not one-by-one.
Tools Needed
Helium 10 Magnet, Claude, your brand guidelines
🔍

11. Listing Audit Agent

Content

Weekly crawl of all your live listings. Checks: keyword indexing, image count/quality score, A+ content present, review score trend, suppression risk, Buy Box %, and content vs. competitor gap. Produces a prioritized fix list ranked by revenue impact.

Why It Matters
Most listings degrade over time. This catches problems before they cost you ranking.
Tools Needed
Amazon SP-API, Helium 10, Firecrawl, Claude
📸

12. Image Brief Generator

Content

Analyzes top-converting competitor images using vision AI. Identifies which lifestyle shots, infographics, size charts, and USP callouts they use. Generates a photographer/designer brief specifying exact shots needed, text overlays, and ordering logic.

Why It Matters
Most sellers guess at images. This tells you exactly what converts in your category.
Tools Needed
Claude Vision, Firecrawl, Figma API
💬

13. Review-to-Copy Feedback Loop

Content

Extracts voice-of-customer language from your 1-3 star reviews and your top competitors' reviews. Identifies unmet expectations and frequently mentioned features. Feeds findings back into listing copy and product development briefs.

Why It Matters
Your buyers tell you exactly what they want. This turns that signal into copy that converts.
Tools Needed
Amazon SP-API reviews endpoint, Claude NLP, Notion
🌍

14. Multilingual Localization Agent

Content

Translates and culturally adapts listings from EN → DE → NL → FR → IT. Not just word-for-word — it adjusts idioms, benefit framing, and compliance language per country. Flags regulatory terms (e.g., German Impressum requirements).

Why It Matters
Bad translations kill conversions in DE and FR. Native-sounding copy from day one.
Tools Needed
Claude, DeepL API, marketplace-specific style guides
Chapter 4
🎧 Customer Service & CX
Scale your customer service without adding headcount. Let AI handle the routine, your team handles the exceptions.
🤖

15. Buyer Message Triage & Response Agent

Customer Service

Reads incoming buyer messages across all marketplaces, classifies intent (return, defect, tracking, complaint, question), drafts a response using your SOP playbooks, and either auto-sends for simple cases or queues for human review for refunds and A-to-Z claims.

Why It Matters
Response time directly impacts seller rating. This ensures every message gets a professional reply within hours.
Tools Needed
Amazon SP-API Messaging, Bol.com Retailer API, Claude, your SOP docs
⭐

16. Negative Review Response Agent

Customer Service

Monitors for new 1-2 star reviews. Within 24h, drafts a brand-appropriate, empathetic public response. For verified buyers, triggers a proactive outreach email offering resolution. Tracks whether reviews get updated.

Why It Matters
30% of negative reviewers update their review after a good response. This recovers ratings automatically.
Tools Needed
SP-API, Bol.com API, Gmail, Claude
📦

17. Return Reason Classifier

Customer Service

Tags every return with a root cause: defect, buyer remorse, wrong item, size issue, expectation mismatch. Builds a monthly report linking return reasons to listing copy gaps or product quality issues. Triggers alerts when a return reason spikes.

Why It Matters
High return rates trigger Amazon investigations. This catches product problems before they become account health issues.
Tools Needed
SP-API Returns API, Claude, your WMS
🛡️

18. A-to-Z / Chargeback Defense Agent

Customer Service

When an A-to-Z claim is filed, auto-assembles the defense package: tracking proof, delivery confirmation, prior buyer messages, order details. Drafts the appeal letter in the format Amazon's team expects.

Why It Matters
A-to-Z claims affect your order defect rate. Winning them keeps your account healthy.
Tools Needed
SP-API, shipping carrier APIs, Claude
Chapter 5
📦 Inventory & Operations
Prevent stockouts, recover money Amazon owes you, and automate the coordination between your warehouse, 3PL, and marketplaces.
🔄

19. Reorder Point Calculator

Operations

Calculates per-SKU reorder points using: current velocity (units/day), supplier lead time, FBA inbound processing time, safety stock buffer, and seasonal multiplier. Fires a Slack alert + draft PO when stock hits the threshold.

Why It Matters
Stockouts kill BSR permanently. Better reorder math = fewer lost sales.
Tools Needed
SP-API Inventory, your ERP, Claude, Slack
💀

20. Stranded & Suppressed Inventory Hunter

Operations

Daily scan for: stranded FBA inventory, suppressed listings, unfulfillable units, and long-term storage fee candidates. Auto-generates removal orders for dead stock or reprices aggressively to sell through before LTS fees hit.

Why It Matters
Suppressed listings don't get found. LTS fees silently erode your margin month after month.
Tools Needed
SP-API, Claude, your repricing rules
💸

21. Shipment Reconciliation Agent

Operations

After every FBA inbound shipment closes, reconciles units shipped vs. units received. Files reimbursement claims for discrepancies within Amazon's claim window (9 months). Tracks claim status and escalates stalled claims.

Why It Matters
Amazon loses 1-3% of inventory in transit. This money is sitting in your Seller Central — claim it.
Tools Needed
SP-API FBA Inbound, Amazon Reimbursement API, Claude
🚚

22. 3PL Coordination Agent

Operations

When an order comes in for a channel not served by FBA (your webshop, Kaufland MFN), sends pick-and-pack instructions to your 3PL via their API, tracks fulfillment, and updates the marketplace with tracking info automatically.

Why It Matters
Manual 3PL coordination is error-prone and doesn't scale. Automate the handoff.
Tools Needed
3PL API (e.g., Byrd, Shiphero), Kaufland API, Claude
Chapter 6
📢 Advertising & Marketing
Lower your TACOS, scale your ad spend profitably, and drive external traffic that earns you Amazon's brand referral bonus.
🎯

23. PPC Campaign Architect

Advertising

Builds Amazon Sponsored Products campaigns from scratch using: your keyword universe, competitor ASINs for targeting, bid landscape data, and budget. Sets up proper campaign structure (exact/phrase/broad + ASIN targeting) with dayparting rules.

Why It Matters
Campaign structure determines your ACOS. A well-built campaign from day one beats months of optimization.
Tools Needed
Amazon Ads API, Helium 10, Claude
🔄

24. Search Term Harvester

Advertising

Weekly: pulls Search Term Reports, identifies converting long-tail terms not yet in exact campaigns, and negative keywords burning spend with zero conversions. Outputs ready-to-upload bulk file for Amazon Ads.

Why It Matters
Search terms change weekly. This ensures you're always harvesting new opportunities before competitors do.
Tools Needed
Amazon Ads API, Claude, Google Sheets
🌐

25. External Traffic Agent

Advertising

Monitors your Brand Referral Bonus credits (Amazon gives 10% back for external traffic). Identifies which products have highest margin + RoI for Meta/Google ads. Drafts ad copy and UTM-tagged landing page URLs. Tracks TACOS impact weekly.

Why It Matters
10% brand referral bonus on every external sale. Free money if you're already driving traffic.
Tools Needed
Amazon Attribution API, Meta Ads API, Claude
🎬

26. Influencer/Creator Outreach Agent

Advertising

Finds micro-influencers on TikTok/Instagram in your product niche with >2% engagement rate and <500k followers. Drafts personalized outreach, tracks responses, and manages product seeding logistics.

Why It Matters
Micro-influencer content converts better than ads and builds long-term brand awareness.
Tools Needed
Firecrawl, Instagram/TikTok APIs, Gmail, Claude
Chapter 7
📈 Analytics & Intelligence
See problems before they hurt your P&L. Get insights that take hours to compile — in minutes.
📊

27. Weekly Business Review (WBR) Generator

Analytics

Every Monday at 06:00, pulls revenue, units, TACOS, BSR, review count, Buy Box %, return rate, and margin per SKU. Compares to prior week, prior month, and YoY. Writes a narrative summary flagging what's up, what's down, and why.

Why It Matters
Most sellers fly blind. This turns raw data into decisions — without opening 10 spreadsheets.
Tools Needed
SP-API, Amazon Ads API, Claude, Notion/Google Slides
🚨

28. Margin Erosion Early Warning

Analytics

Monitors your net margin per ASIN daily. When margin drops >3pp vs. 30-day average, auto-diagnoses cause: fee increase, price drop, COGS change, or ad spend spike. Sends alert with root cause and suggested action.

Why It Matters
Margin erosion is silent until it's catastrophic. Catching it at 3% vs 15% is the difference between a quick fix and a crisis.
Tools Needed
SP-API Finance, Ads API, your ERP, Claude
📈

29. Category Ranking Tracker

Analytics

Tracks your BSR in main category + 3 subcategories, and your top 5 competitors' BSRs, daily. Detects when a competitor is running a promo (sudden BSR spike) so you can decide whether to match or hold.

Why It Matters
Competitor promos hit your BSR. Knowing in real-time lets you respond — not react after the fact.
Tools Needed
Keepa API, Claude
📋

30. Regulatory Change Monitor

Analytics

Watches EU/NL/DE regulatory feeds, Amazon policy update pages, and Bol.com seller news for changes affecting your product categories (CE marking, GPSR, battery regulations, labeling rules). Sends digest with impact assessment.

Why It Matters
Non-compliance fines are expensive. Regulatory changes are predictable — if you monitor them.
Tools Needed
Firecrawl, EUR-Lex RSS, Amazon Policy pages, Claude
Chapter 8
🛡️ Account Health & Compliance
Stay in business. These agents prevent the problems that get sellers suspended.
❤️‍🩹

31. Account Health Dashboard Agent

Compliance

Daily check of Amazon Account Health: ODR, Late Shipment Rate, Valid Tracking Rate, Policy violations. Triggers playbook if any metric is within 20% of the threshold. For Bol.com: tracks NPS score and on-time delivery rate.

Why It Matters
Account suspensions are expensive and hard to recover from. Prevention beats cure.
Tools Needed
SP-API Account Health API, Bol.com Retailer API, Claude
⚔️

32. ASIN Hijacking Alert

Compliance

Monitors your listings for unauthorized 3P sellers (hijackers). When detected, immediately sends cease-and-desist template to the seller, files a brand registry complaint, and documents the incident. Tracks resolution.

Why It Matters
Hijackers steal the Buy Box and your revenue. Fast action = fast recovery.
Tools Needed
SP-API, Amazon Brand Registry API, Gmail, Claude
